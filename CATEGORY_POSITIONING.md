# CapyMatch - Category Positioning

## What Category Is CapyMatch Creating?

**CapyMatch is building the first Recruiting Operating System.**

Not a CRM. Not an athlete profile tool. Not a communication platform.

An operating system that coordinates the entire support infrastructure around every athlete in a program—making recruiting less chaotic, more transparent, and more successful.

---

## The Problem

### Today's Reality for Club Programs:

**Coaches are flying blind.**
- Which athletes are losing momentum? Unknown until it's too late.
- Who needs help right now? Requires manual detective work.
- Is the family aligned with the coach? Often unclear.
- Did anyone follow up after that showcase? Maybe, maybe not.
- Which athletes are falling through the cracks? No way to know systematically.

**Families feel unsupported.**
- "Is our coach really helping us?"
- "We haven't heard from them in weeks."
- "I don't know what to do next."
- "Are we on track?"

**Athletes stall or commit to the wrong school.**
- Momentum dies from inattention
- Opportunities are missed
- Families navigate alone
- Coaches can't scale their support

**The root cause:**  
Recruiting support is invisible, uncoordinated, and reactive.

Coaches have tools for profiles and messaging, but **no system for coordinating support at scale.**

## What Makes CapyMatch Different

### vs. Traditional Recruiting CRMs

**Traditional CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot-style):**
- Built for sales pipelines
- Focused on contact management
- Designed for individual reps tracking deals
- Reactive: you query it for information
- Transactional: focus is on closing
- Single-player: one user, one workflow

**CapyMatch Recruiting Operating System:**
- Built for coordinated athlete support
- Focused on momentum and intervention
- Designed for coaches coordinating families, schools, and programs
- Proactive: it tells you what needs attention
- Relational: focus is on supporting humans
- Multi-player: coaches, families, advisors working together

**Key Difference:**  
A CRM asks *"What's the status?"*  
CapyMatch asks *"Who needs help and what should we do?"*

- Built for individual athletes and families
- Profile creation and exposure

- Direct athlete-to-college communication

**CapyMatch (Integrated Athlete + Coach System):**
- Built for programs that support athletes

- Assumes a team approach to recruiting success

**Key Difference:**  
Athlete tools say *"Here's how to recruit yourself."*  
CapyMatch says *"Here's how your entire support system works together."*

## The Core Insight

**Recruiting is not a solo sport.**

It's a coordination problem:
- Between athlete, family, club coach, high school coach, and advisors
- Between showcases, communication, follow-ups, and decisions
- Between momentum, blockers, and opportunities

Today, that coordination happens through:
- Text messages (lost)
- Email threads (buried)
- Verbal updates (forgotten)
- Spreadsheets (outdated)
- Memory (unreliable)

**CapyMatch makes coordination visible, systematic, and scalable.**

## Category Definition

**Recruiting Operating System (ROS)**

A platform that:
1. **Coordinates support** across all stakeholders (coaches, families, athletes, advisors)
2. **Surfaces priorities** proactively (who needs help, what changed, what's next)
3. **Structures workflows** for recruiting contexts (events, advocacy, support pods)
4. **Preserves intelligence** (relationship memory, momentum signals, outcomes)
5. **Operates at program scale** (not just individual athletes)

CapyMatch is the first Recruiting Operating System built specifically for club programs and the families they serve.
